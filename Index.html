❤️ Our Love Story ❤️
Tumhari aankhon mein aisi gehraai hai,
jaise har raaz meri mohabbat ka chhupa ho unmein 🥰
Iss tasveer mein sirf do chehre nahi,
ek poori duniya hai jisme sirf tum aur main hain 💫
Bas ek muskaan, aur yeh pal hamesha ke liye khaas ban gaya 💫
Phoolon mein bhi itni khushboo nahi jitni tumhari muskaan mein hai 💫
Tumhein dekh kar lagta hai ke khushi bhi ek shakl le sakti hai 💫
Yeh din sirf yaad nahi,
ek mehsoos hone waali khushi thi
jo sirf tumhare saath hi mumkin thi 💫
Us din toh mazaa itna aaya...
lag raha tha date pe main gaya hoon,
lekin dil toh tum le gayi 😉❤️ 💫
Tumhe dekh kar toh dil bhi confuse ho gaya…
dhadakna chahe ya ruk jana 😍 💫
Jab tumhara haath pakadta hoon,
lagta hai saari pareshaniyaan kahin kho jaati hain…
jaise sirf tum hi ho aur main hoon, aur duniya ekdum theek hai
❤️✨ 💫
Tujh bin adhura hoon main, yeh tu jaan le,
Har saans tujhmein hai, yeh pehchaan le.
Khuda se bas yahi dua hai har dafa,
Kabhi na chhod ke jaaye tu, yeh armaan le.
Tum meri muskurahat ka sabab ho,
Tum meri duaon ka jawab ho,
Zindagi ki har subah tumse ho,
Aur har raat tumhare khwab ho. 💖
